### 19. Inference-First vs Training-First Demand Shift
Rubin's architecture is heavily optimized for inference (FP4, NVLink 6). If hyperscaler demand shifts earlier in the cycle than expected (e.g., frontier training still requires Hopper/Blackwell-class memory bandwidth), Rubin's inference-optimized design may see uneven adoption.

### 20. Competition: AMD MI400 Timing Risk
