RISK: NVIDIA NVDA Research — Risk, Blocker, Assumption, and Mitigation Map

## EXECUTION RISKS

### 1. Rubin Production Delay (HIGH → CRITICAL)
- Rubin (R200) slipping from Q2 2026 toward H2 2026; 2026 unit target cut from 2M → 1.5M.
- Root Causes: HBM4 shortages & extended validation (SK Hynix/Micron prioritizing Blackwell); ConnectX-9 NIC transitions; higher power draw; liquid cooling re-optimization; TSMC CoWoS bottleneck; recent tape-out redesigns.
- Impact: AI infra rollout slows; customers forced toward Blackwell; RTX 60 gaming pushback to 2028.
- Mitigation: Plan Blackwell-heavy transition strategy; hedge with AMD MI450 for inference workloads.

### 2. HBM Memory Shortage — Persistent Through 2027+ (HIGH)
- HBM4 supply constrained; no quick scaling given unprecedented AI datacenter demand.
- Impact: Inflates costs, caps GPU production volumes for all advanced NVIDIA chips.
- Mitigation: Monitor SK Hynix/Micron allocation; model scenarios with <1M Rubin units in 2026.

### 3. Geopolitical / Taiwan Strait (CRITICAL — Long-Term)
- 100% of advanced NVIDIA GPUs manufactured in Taiwan; US export controls on China tightening.
- Impact: Any Taiwan event = catastrophic supply disruption; China market increasingly inaccessible.
- Mitigation: Intel 18A node (Feynman GPUs ~2028) as diversification; track TSMC Arizona进度.

## DEPENDENCY RISKS

### 4. TSMC CoWoS Packaging Bottleneck (HIGH → 2026)
- Advanced packaging shifting from wafer limit to CoWoS limit; 120-130K wafers/month projected end-2026, still insufficient.
- Impact: Constrains Rubin, Blackwell, and future GPU yields equally.

### 5. Single-Source TSMC Manufacturing (CRITICAL)
- No alternative foundry currently viable for advanced AI GPUs; Intel 18A/Samsung 2nm early-stage.
- Impact: Any TSMC disruption (natural disaster, political) cascades immediately to NVIDIA.
- Mitigation: NVIDIA-Intel partnership ($5B, 18A node) is strategic hedge; Feynman (2028) as first non-TSMC tape-out.

### 6. Vera Rubin Rack Forecast Overstatement (MEDIUM)
- Initial Vera Rubin rack forecasts of 12,000-14,000 downgraded to ~6,000; Blackwell fills gap.
- Impact: Downstream AI infrastructure planning may be built on inflated assumptions.
